Understanding Our 14-Yard Dumpster Size

Perfect Size for Most Projects

Our 14-yard dumpster holds approximately 14 cubic yards of material—equivalent to about 4-5 pickup truck loads. With dimensions of roughly 14 feet long, 7.5 feet wide, and 4 feet tall, it fits comfortably in most driveways without blocking traffic or requiring special permits.

What Can You Fit?

This versatile size is ideal for kitchen or bathroom remodels, garage cleanouts, roofing projects (up to 2,000 sq ft), deck removal, estate cleanouts, and general household junk removal. At 4,000 lbs capacity included in our $299 base price, it handles everything from construction debris and old furniture to yard waste and renovation materials. Need more capacity? Additional weight is available at competitive rates.
