Rental Details

Prohibited Items

For the safety of your home and community, the following items must not be disposed of in the dumpster:

  • Batteries
  • Liquids of any kind
  • Asbestos-containing materials
  • Electronic waste (e.g., computers, monitors)
  • Motor vehicle parts or fluids
  • Yard waste (leaves, branches, etc.)
  • Dirt, soil, sand, or concrete

Improper disposal may result in additional fees or penalties. If unsure about an item, please contact us for guidance.

Fees & Penalties

  • Overfilling Fee: $50 (if contents exceed the top rim)
  • Additional Weight: $0.03 per pound (above 4,000 pounds)
  • Special Handling Items (Extra Charges Apply):
    • Appliances: $25 each
    • Tires: $10 each
    • Tube TVs: $30 each
  • Cancellation Fee: A $25 fee applies to cancellations made within one (1) day of the scheduled rental date.
  • Blocked Access: If the dumpster is not accessible at the time of pickup (e.g., blocked by a vehicle), no fee will be charged, but pickup will be rescheduled at the next available time.
