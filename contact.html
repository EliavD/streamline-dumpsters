Meet Our Expert Team

At Streamline Dumpsters, we wear many hats. Actually, I wear all the hats. Meet the crew:

Eli - Customer Service Expert

When you call Streamline Dumpsters, you'll speak directly with Eli—our friendly, knowledgeable customer service specialist who happens to also be the owner. With years of experience answering phones (and occasionally putting them on speaker while driving a dumpster truck), Eli ensures every customer gets personalized attention. No phone trees, no hold music, just honest answers and maybe a dad joke or two.

Eli - Delivery & Pickup Specialist

Meet Eli, our top-rated delivery driver and logistics coordinator. He's mastered the art of placing dumpsters exactly where you need them without damaging your driveway. Fun fact: He's the same Eli you talked to on the phone! When he's not delivering dumpsters with surgical precision, he's picking them up just as carefully. His secret? Treating every driveway like it's his own (because customer satisfaction really matters when you're a one-person show).

Eli - Financial Director

Introducing Eli, our meticulous CFO who keeps the books balanced and prices transparent. After a long day of answering phones and driving around Central Ohio, he trades his work boots for a calculator to ensure Streamline Dumpsters stays financially sound. His philosophy? Simple, honest pricing with no hidden fees—because trying to remember complicated pricing structures while wearing three different hats is exhausting. The $299 price tag isn't just competitive; it's easy to remember at 2 AM while doing paperwork.

Yes, it's the same guy. But hey, that means you'll never get the runaround—Eli handles everything from your first call to final pickup. One person, one promise: exceptional service every time.
